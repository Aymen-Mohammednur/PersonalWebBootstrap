Book - The Alchemist

The Alchemist, for most people, is quite light to read, yet gives such a profound perspective to life, for a book that light. It tells a tale of generic premise: a young man chasing his dream, with not so generic experience, thought, and perspective.

It is the story of a young guy who leaves his house in search of his dream. On his way, he meets many people who, knowingly or unknowingly, teach him something.

The entire book focuses on one belief only - “If you really want something, the whole universe conspires to help you”.
